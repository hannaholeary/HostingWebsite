Undergraduate Degree Final Year Project

The day which marked one of my proudest accomplishments during my incredible time studying Criminal Justice and Psychology at The University of Limerick.

I was delighted to submit my Final Year Project which explored the possibility for change in legislation in relation to euthanasia given the change in legislation due to the abortion referendum. I also had the pleasure of having assistance from my supervisor Paul McCutcheon.

I have had the best years of my life studying at UL, and I am so grateful for the experiences and opportunities I have gained there.

Although this last semester has been challenging due to these unforeseen circumstances, I am proud of how I adapted to such adversity.

I have thoroughly enjoyed studying under both Schools of Law and Psychology at the University of Limerick. I am looking forward to incorporating the unique skillset and perspectives I have acquired to my future endeavours.
