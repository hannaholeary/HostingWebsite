Postgrad Graduation Statement

Class of 2020

Graduation: BA. Joint Honours Degree in Criminal Justice and Psychology

I am over the moon to have graduated with a degree in Criminal Justice & Psychology from The University of Limerick.

The last few of my undergrad were extremely challenging given the effects of the worldwide pandemic. I am most proud of achieving an Honours Degree despite the challenges of Covid-19 to the examination process and to law and psychology research.

I am immensely grateful for the time I spent at and the opportunities afforded to me by The University of Limerick. My Erasmus experience at Radboud University Nijmegen and my CO-OP work placement in #Barcelona were two unique experiences which have been major highlights to my university experience.

UL and all of the people I have had the pleasure of meeting there including lecturers, coordinations, mentors, and classmates has helped shape the person I am today.

I am excited for the future and I am looking forward to exploring pathways in my career which will allow me to further my interest in and passion for Criminal Law and Psychology.
